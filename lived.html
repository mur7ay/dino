Area Lived

North America

T. Rex fossils have been discovered on two continents, primarily on the Americas and Asia. The most common place for them to live was in the area considered Texas, Utah, Montana, Wyoming, and including some areas of Canada.

Asia

T Rex fossils has also been found in a same area of China known as Mongolia. Due to evolution, the T Rex found in China looked slightly different with an extended, slender snoute than those traditional (and most popular) ones found in North America.
